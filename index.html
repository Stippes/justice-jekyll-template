Alethia uncovers how AI systems shape your choices and emotions.

We blend behavioral science and technical analysis to help you take control of your digital life.

Our goal is not to demonize AI but to shed light on manipulative design practices that erode autonomy and bypass consent.

Mission Highlights

  • Educate citizens about psychological vulnerabilities exploited by digital platforms and AI.
  • Analyze and expose manipulative design via empirical research and LLM-based testing.
  • Support policymakers with plain-language explainers and edge-case analysis.
  • Provide hands-on tools for chatbot testing and digital self-protection.

How AI Shapes Your Online Experience

  • Reinforcement loops: Platforms like TikTok learn to exploit your attention patterns.
  • Emotional manipulation: Chatbots simulate empathy to build dependency.
  • UX dark patterns: Design choices push you into accepting cookies, clicking ads, or making unplanned purchases.
