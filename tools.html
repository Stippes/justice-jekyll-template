Even the most advanced AI system can be resisted—if you understand how it works on you. This Toolkit offers hands-on strategies to reduce the influence of manipulative algorithms on your attention, emotions, and decisions.

🧭 Digital Habits Checklist

  • Install the “One Sec” app: Adds a 1-second delay before opening addictive apps, helping break unconscious habits.
  • Turn off autoplay: Disable automatic video playback on YouTube, Netflix, and TikTok to regain agency over your time.
  • Use greyscale mode: Set your phone screen to grayscale during evenings or work hours to reduce visual temptation.
  • Remove algorithmic feeds: Replace recommendation-driven apps with chronological versions (e.g., RSS readers, unbundled YouTube).
  • Turn off personalized ads: Visit your Google and Meta ad settings to minimize micro-targeting.

🛡️ Recommended Browser Extensions

  • uBlock Origin: Blocks ads and trackers to reduce attention-hijacking noise.
  • Privacy Badger: Automatically learns and blocks invisible trackers.
  • Consent-O-Matic: Auto-fills GDPR cookie forms to say “no” to tracking—every time.
  • DF Tube: Hides YouTube’s recommendations and comments, letting you focus on your intention.
  • News Feed Eradicator: Replaces social media feeds with motivational quotes or neutral backgrounds.

🧠 Emotional Awareness Tips

Manipulative systems often target emotional triggers—especially outrage, fear, and FOMO. Here's how to resist:

  • Pause before clicking or reacting to emotionally charged content.
  • Ask yourself: “Who benefits if I act on this impulse?”
  • Use journaling or brief mindfulness exercises when feeling hijacked by digital emotion loops.
  • Don’t engage with bots: always verify who (or what) you’re talking to.

💬 Safe Practices When Using Chatbots

  • Don't overshare: Remember that some systems use your inputs to personalize or profile you.
  • Use clear boundaries: Avoid long emotional conversations with bots. They're not your friend—even if they seem like it.
  • Take regular breaks: Use short, timed sessions with tools like “Focus Keeper” or Pomodoro apps.
  • Ask “who trained this bot?”: Check the company, their business model, and their AI disclosures.
