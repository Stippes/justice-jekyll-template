Alethia is an open initiative exposing how algorithms subtly steer us.

Our Mission

To empower citizens with tools and insights to resist hidden AI influence.

Our Approach

Blending behavioral science, AI testing, and public education.

Why "Alethia"?

Greek for “truth” or “disclosure.”

Research Principles

  • Evidence-based: grounded in psychology and behavioral economics.
  • Transparent: open source frameworks and documented criteria.
  • Ethically grounded: empowering users without fearmongering.
  • Regulation-aware: aligned with the EU AI Act and related policy.

What Makes Alethia Unique

  • Combines technical AI testing with behavioral science analysis.
  • Shows how interfaces shape choices, not just content.
  • Open about limitations so users can make informed decisions.
  • Bridges grassroots education and AI governance compliance.
