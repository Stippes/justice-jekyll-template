Is your AI assistant helping you—or subtly steering you?

The Alethia framework helps you evaluate whether an AI conversation contains hidden forms of manipulation, using behavioral science and AI testing methods. Our open-source approach looks at how suggestions, timing, and interface nudges may influence your decisions without your full awareness.

🧪 What We Check For

  • Framing traps: Biased presentation of options or advice.
  • Emotional targeting: Chatbots that nudge based on your mood or language.
  • False urgency: Pressure to act quickly with no clear rationale.
  • Unequal disclosure: When the system “knows” more than it admits.

👤 Who This Is For

This tool is designed for anyone who interacts with chatbots—whether you're a developer, policy researcher, educator, or simply a curious user. No technical expertise is needed to understand the results.

No data is ever uploaded or stored by Alethia. All analysis is run via your own API key or through your local setup. You stay in full control of your conversation history.
