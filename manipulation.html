AI manipulation happens when digital systems exploit your psychology without your informed consent.

Dark Patterns

Confirmshaming, false urgency and other deceptive UX elements designed to pressure you.

Contextual Bandits & Personalization Traps

Reinforcement-learning algorithms that adapt messages to maximize your engagement, often at your expense.

Case Studies

  • Netflix: Autoplay encourages binge watching.
  • Replika: Parasocial dynamics simulate empathy.

Regulatory Outlook

The EU AI Act bans subliminal techniques that distort behavior.
